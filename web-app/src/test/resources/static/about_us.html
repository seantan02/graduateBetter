How We Started

We started because our founder has difficulity figuring out what courses to take in order to obtain all majors he wanted: Computer Science, Data Science. He also knows that doing these 2 majors puts him in a position that with just a few more classes, he will be able to obtain several more majors/minors. Thus, he wishes there is a tool like Graduate Better that can help a freshy figure out what majors they could aim for.
